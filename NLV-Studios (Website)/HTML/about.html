About NLV Studios

NLV Studios was founded in 2025 in the heart of Tzaneen, Limpopo, South Africa. Born from a passion for sound and accessibility, we set out to create a space where aspiring musicians, DJs, and small studios could find professional-grade equipment without breaking the bank.

Our Mission

To empower creators by providing affordable, high-quality studio gear that supports every stage of their musical journey from bedroom beats to broadcast-ready productions.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s most trusted online destination for innovative and affordable studio equipment, rooted in community and creativity.

Who We Serve

  • Independent music producers and DJs aged 16–40
  • Small recording studios and music schools
  • Aspiring musicians building home studios

Why Tzaneen?

We’re proud to represent Limpopo’s creative spirit. Tzaneen is more than our home. It’s our heartbeat. NLV Studios is here to amplify local talent and connect it to the world.
